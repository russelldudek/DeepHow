Where leadership matters

The work lives in the contradictions.

Partner trust comes from making tradeoffs explicit—not from pretending a platform can make them disappear.

Practice judgmentversusFirm-wide reuse

Standardize reusable controls and components while preserving legitimate matter and practice exceptions.

Speed and leverageversusPrivilege and defensibility

Scale review, authority, evidence, and access controls with consequence and sensitivity.

Model capabilityversusKnowledge readiness

Advance workflows and the source content, taxonomy, ownership, and freshness that make them trustworthy.

Pilot enthusiasmversusPortfolio concentration

Focus the team on use cases with accountable owners, measurable outcomes, and a credible path to adoption.
