# Campaign Audit

Campaign state: building until main-branch, generated-PDF, live-route, and exact-publication checks are complete.

## Factual integrity
- Candidate claims map to verified evidence.
- No formal product-management tenure, production ML ownership, unsupported platform scale, internal DeepHow architecture, or invented metrics are claimed.
- DeepHow-specific architectural content is identified as candidate hypothesis or illustrative decision model.

## Narrative continuity
- Mandate: turn four product lines into one compounding platform.
- Thesis: Make physical work addressable.
- Operating artifact: Work Coordinate System / Work Address Review.
- Entry sequence: Read → Define → Prove → Install.
- Resume, cover letter, brief, plan, and review reinforce the same argument without copying full prose.

## Brand and independence
- Official logo source and product-image provenance documented.
- Official/source-sampled tokens distinguished from candidate-original accents.
- Company identity remains separate from the candidate thesis and is paired with independent-candidate language.

## Required verification
- Source and binary manifest on `main`.
- Official logo committed locally.
- PDF page counts and rendered-page inspection.
- Desktop, laptop, tablet, mobile, keyboard, interaction, reset, reduced-motion, link, image, and overflow checks.
- Direct PDF downloads and reciprocal resume/cover-letter navigation.
- Candidate-facing confidentiality scan.
- Live GitHub Pages exact-head verification.
