Re: Product Director, Physical AI Platform

DeepHow's opportunity is to turn Knowledge Capture & Transfer, Operational Output Verification, Live SOP Verification, and Time & Motion AI into one compounding Physical AI platform. I would be excited to lead the decisions that make that possible: what must be shared, what should remain federated, and how customers reach verified value faster.

My background is uniquely grounded where software, machines, quality systems, operators, and customer commitments meet. At Vape-Jet, I lead AI-first operating transformation across production, quality, support, engineering issue flow, enterprise systems, telemetry, and a field fleet of approximately 1,000 software-enabled robots. Earlier, I led high-reliability engineering and manufacturing at Compunetics, customer-backward launch mechanisms at Amazon, regulated autonomous drone operations at ZeusVu, and AI-readiness and workflow design through DudeWorth.

That experience shapes my product judgment. I start with the customer outcome, make evidence and human authority explicit, run bounded experiments, and instrument trust and value. The goal is not another compelling AI demonstration; it is a capability that works in real operating conditions and becomes easier to deploy, govern, and improve.

In the first 120 days, I would listen before prescribing, map the product lines to a shared work and evidence model, identify where platform leverage is already emerging, and prove one bounded cross-product use case with a measurable customer outcome. I would then turn what works into clear product contracts, ownership, evaluation, and operating cadence.

I made this thinking visible at russelldudek.github.io/DeepHow/. The candidate vision includes a Work Address Resolver, a 120-day entry plan, and a Work Address Review. It is a sample of how I frame the mandate and make product decisions, not a claim about DeepHow's internal processes.

I would welcome the opportunity to discuss how my operating background and product judgment can help DeepHow turn its product portfolio into a coherent, enterprise-grade Physical AI platform.
